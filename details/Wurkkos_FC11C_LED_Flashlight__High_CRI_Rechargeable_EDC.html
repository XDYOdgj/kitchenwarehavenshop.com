Wurkkos FC11C LED Flashlight

A durable, high-performance flashlight designed for everyday carry and emergency situations.

With 1200 lumens of bright light and an impressive 155-meter beam distance, this flashlight is built to handle any challenge. Whether you're hiking, camping, or simply need reliable illumination during the night, the FC11C provides the power and clarity you need.

The natural white 4000K color temperature ensures vivid lighting and accurate color representation, making it ideal for both practical and professional use. Its professional-grade LED technology delivers consistent brightness without overheating or flickering.

Featuring a magnetic tailcap, the FC11C can be securely attached to metal surfaces or left upright for easy access. This hands-free design is perfect for multitasking, outdoor activities, or emergency preparedness.

This waterproof, rechargeable flashlight is rated IP67, meaning it can withstand rain, splashes, and dust. It's built with an aluminum alloy body to ensure strength and longevity, making it a dependable companion in tough conditions.

Designed with a type-C compatible charging system, the FC11C charges quickly and efficiently, with a built-in indicator to show when it's fully charged. Its compact size makes it easy to carry in your pocket or bag, ensuring you're never without light.

Whether you're looking for a flashlight for EDC (Every Day Carry), outdoor adventures, or emergency situations, the Wurkkos FC11C delivers unmatched reliability and brightness. Lighting up your world with a powerful, professional-grade design.
