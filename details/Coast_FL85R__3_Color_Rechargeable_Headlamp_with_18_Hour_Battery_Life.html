Coast FL85R Rechargeable Headlamp

The Coast FL85R is a perfect blend of functionality and style, designed to meet the needs of adventurers, workers, and everyday users. With its compact and durable build, it's ideal for both exploration and precision tasks.

This headlamp offers an impressive 18-hour battery life, making it a reliable companion for long days in the field. The built-in USB rechargeable design ensures you can charge it anytime, anywhere—no more worrying about running out of power.

Choose from three vibrant LED color options: white, red, and green. Whether you're hiking, working under low light, or using it at home, the right color can make all the difference in visibility and performance.

The adjustable flood and spot modes provide flexibility for various lighting needs. The bright flood mode delivers strong illumination for close-up tasks, while the long-range spot beam helps you see further ahead with clarity.

Featuring smear-free optics, the FL85R ensures sharp and clear LED performance without glare or distortion. This makes it perfect for activities that require precision, such as camping, climbing, or nighttime work.

Why Choose the FL85R?

  • 18-hour battery life – Stay powered through long adventures or heavy use.
  • Rechargeable via USB – Easy to charge and eco-friendly.
  • Triple-color LED – White, red, and green for different lighting scenarios.
  • Adjustable flood and spot modes – Switch between bright flood and long-distance spot for versatile use.
  • Smear-free optics – Sharp, distortion-free illumination for precise tasks.
  • 1000 lumens output – Bright and powerful light for all your needs.
