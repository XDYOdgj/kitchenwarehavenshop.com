Durable IPX6 Waterproof Headlamp – 70 Hours of Light

Stay illuminated in any condition with this exceptional waterproof headlamp. Built for rugged use, it offers 70 hours of battery life on the lowest setting, ensuring you never run out of light during long outdoor trips.

Equipped with a motion sensor, it automatically turns on when you move, making it perfect for exploring in the dark or navigating unfamiliar terrain without the hassle of flipping switches. And with a 2.5-hour full charge, you get ready for action faster than ever.
